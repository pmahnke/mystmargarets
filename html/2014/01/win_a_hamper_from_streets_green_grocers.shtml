Win a hamper from Streets Green Grocers

Chase Buchanan’s NEW COMPETITION IS HERE!!!

How would you like to win a FREE hamper of the healthiest, most colourful and most exotic selection of fruit and veg from St Margaret’s favourite Green Grocers? Streets Green Grocers are offering one lucky winner a nice, tasty hamper full of vitamins and goodness!

Have you spent January detoxing after an indulgent Christmas? Did you cut out all alcoholic drinks for the duration and make it through the ‘dry-athalon’? Did you get your five-a-day every day? No? Neither did we! Fortunately, St Margarets’ favourite greengrocer is willing to help us out a bit by boosting our vitamin intake, giving us some natural energy and putting some goodness in us with a fantastic hamper full of fruit and vegetable!

Streets florist & greengrocer is a well-established family run business that operates in the St Margarets, East Twickenham and Richmond area. They specialize in creating floral arrangements using the finest fresh seasonal flowers and foliage’s available to us. They also offer the most sumptuous fresh fruit & vegetable boxes to satisfy fruity cravings or to add to your favourite recipes!

The residents and locals will often be seen in St Margarets village sipping from an assortment of fruity smoothies- the most popular drinks in the area! Pick your favourite flavour, whether it’s ‘Very Berry’ or ‘Mango Passion’, and enjoy a tasty treat whilst getting your five a day! If you’re stuck for dinner or are out and about in need of a snack- pop into Streets-it’s the only place in the village that stocks a selection of fresh fruit and vegetable!

Streets florist and greengrocer is the perfect place for a light, healthy snack or a selection of fresh, exotic ingredients and with Valentine’s Day just around the corner it is definitely worth checking out their beautiful, freshly cut, handpicked luxury floral bouquets all wrapped up in a nice bow for that special someone!
Their friendly and reliable service also includes same day free delivery within a radius of 3 miles.

To be in with a chance of winning, simply follow these steps:

1. Like the Chase Buchanan Facebook page (if you haven’t already) by clicking here
2. Comment on the competition post with your name and your town
3. “SHARE” the post on your timeline (by clicking the “share” button underneath the post).

GOOD LUCK!
