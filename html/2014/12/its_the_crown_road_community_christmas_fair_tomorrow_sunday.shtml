It's the Crown Road Community Christmas Fair tomorrow, Sunday 7th December, 11am - 4pm!!!!!

Look forward to seeing you there for a wide range of stalls that’ll take care of your Christmas gift shopping, children’s entertainers including our stiltwalkers and balloon modeller, fairground rides, inflatables, a Strong Man contest, pony rides down Bridle Lane, musicians and carol-singing, mulled wine, mince pies, live ice-scultping (have a look at the video below!), real-life reindeer and of course… SANTA!!

Thank you to our sponsors for making all this possible: Chase Buchanan, RFU, St George plc, St Margarets Tavern, Fitz-Gibbon and Dolly Rock.

Thanks also to Susan of the Secret Wardrobe (Crown Road) and to Pilates Plus for their help!

And after the fair.. late night shopping on Crown Rd on Thursday 11th December, with a number of your fave shops open ‘til 8pm!
