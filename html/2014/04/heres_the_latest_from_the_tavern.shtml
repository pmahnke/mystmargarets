Here's the latest from the Tavern

Here’s the latest news from the St Margarets Tavern:

“Time for a quick update on what we have coming up at ‘The Tavern’ … but first, take a look at our fabulous new menu (attached).

We will be launching this on Wednesday night, but you get a sneak preview…

Book a table now if you would like to be one of the first to try out these mouth-watering dishes!

Easter Sunday

Sunday 20th April

All the fun of Sunday, but with added chocolate!

Book your table now. Everyone gets mini eggs!

We will also be serving Roasts on Easter Monday, so if you missed out (or just fancy another!) you know where to come!

Sugar T & The Swells: Friday April 25th

Can you believe that we are already half way through yet another month?

The plus side if this is that it means it is almost time to party St Margarets style!

Bring a smile. Bring your dancin’ feet. Sugar T & the Swells are in the house!


Traditional Ale & Craft Lager Festival: Wednesday 7th - Sunday 11th May

Featuring five hand pulls plus six gravity fed ales poured straight from the cask and a selection of bottled craft lagers, this will be five days of beer and fun.

  • Pie & Ale Night * Meet the Brewers * Live Music * Taster Paddles * 10% Discount to CAMRA Members * plus much more *

Music & Movies Quiz: Thursday 22nd May

With Twickenham Film Studios across the road and lying at the heart of an area steeped in music history, it seems only right that we hold a ‘Movies & Music Quiz Night’

Hosted by our very own ‘El Shipp’, this promises to be a fun filled night with a few surprises thrown in for good measure.

Entry will be £2 per player. £1 of this goes into the ‘Winner-takes-all-prize-Jackpot’.

The other £1 will be donated to a very worthy children’s charity. We would also like to hold a Charity Raffle on the same night. If any of you would like to donate a prize, please get in touch

Twickenham Rugby Dates

There are quite a few big games coming up at Twickenham over the next few weeks. If you would like to book a table for lunch before any of these, then please drop us a line.

Saturday April 19th (St Georges Day): The Stinger, Wasps v Gloucester

Saturday 26th April: Heineken Cup Semi Final, Saracens v Clermont Auvergne

Saturday May 3rd: Babcock Trophy, Army v Navy

Saturday 31st May: Aviva Premiership Cup Final

BYO Mondays

Monday night is BYO Wine Night in our dining room.

No strings. No catches. No corkage. All you need to do is order main courses from our dinner menu and enjoy a bottle of your favourite wine.

Prix Fixe Dinner Menu

From Monday to Friday between 6-8pm we run a fantastic value Prix Fixe Dinner Menu.

Two Courses £13.50. Three Courses £17.50

Combine that with BYO Monday and you have an incredible bargain to start the week!

Set Price Lunch Menu

We also run an excellent value Set Price Lunch Menu.

Two Courses £12. Three Courses £15

Crayfish & smoked salmon risotto followed by Vanilla cheesecake with strawberry compote anyone?!

Sunday Night Quiz

St Margarets Tavern was made for Sundays!

Amazing roasts * Fierce Bloody Marys * Newspapers * Afternoon sport

All this, and then there’s the Quiz Night in the evening.

Only £1 per player. Bar tab for the winning team and a bottle of wine for the best team name … and good natured, competitive fun for all!

So there you have it. Lots to look forward to.

See you on the terrace for a bite to eat and a glass of something cold soon.

Mitch & The St Margarets Crew!

x”
