Win a Cadbury Hamper by keeping-up with Cutters!

To celebrate their new Facebook and Twitter feeds, Cutters of 28 Crown Road are giving-away this gorgeous and delicious Cadbury Hamper!

Just ‘Like’ their Facebook page at www.facebook.com/CuttersTW1 or ‘Follow’ their Twitter feed at twitter.com/Cutters_TW1 between now and 31st December, and you’ll be entered into a prize-draw.

We”ll anounce the winner picked at random on January 2nd 2016!

Good luck everyone!
