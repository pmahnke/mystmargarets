Here's to one of St Margarets' unsung heroes...

Rita, of Cutters, 28 Crown Rd!

Not only is she the heart of “the shop that sells everything”, Rita picks up the slack when Cutters’ manager Adam is away helping to organise the Crown Rd Fairs with Vintage and Velvet’s Amanda.

And this week we’re extra appreciative of Rita as she’s been working extra-hard to cover for staff holidays and illness!

Rita, we salute you!

Stop-in and say hi to one of St Margarets’ favourite scousers from 8am - 7pm Monday to Saturday, and 9am - 7pm Sunday.
