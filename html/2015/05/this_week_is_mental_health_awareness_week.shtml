This Week is Mental Health Awareness Week

so we’d like to make you aware of the fantastic work the Depression Alliance Twickenham team do to help those suffering with depression.

This support group’s meetings are held twice a month on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday, 7.30pm to 9.00pm at Holy Trinity Church, The Green, Twickenham, TW2 5TU.

Please contact them before attending a session, you can find out more via their website here

Group facilitator Alasdair will also be raising awareness at the Crown Road Community Fair on Sunday 7th June.
