St Margarets' Village Planning Meetings

While we’re all still in the political mood, here’s notice of London Borough of Richmond upon Thames’s Village Plan consultation meetings, where you can air your views on the issues effecting St Margarets, and help direct the council’s efforts:

https://consultation.richmond.gov.uk/consultation/your-stmargarets

Saturday May 16th: Drop-In Event…
An opportunity to meet with the village planning team and share your thoughts on your local village plan.
At Twickenham Studios, The Barons, Twickenham TW1 2AW, any time between 10am and 4pm.

Sunday May 17th: Walkabout…
An opportunity to walk around your area with the village planning team and share your thoughts on your local village plan.
