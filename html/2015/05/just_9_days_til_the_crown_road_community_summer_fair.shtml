Just 9 days 'til the Crown Road Community Summer Fair!

Brought to you by The St Margarets Traders Association!

Animal-lovers are in for a treat as there’ll be a petting zoo on Crown Rd! Come along and befriend all manner of farmyard creatures!

The lady responsible for organising the fair is our very own Amanda, of Vintage and Velvet, 6 Crown Road: Thank you Amanda!

See you all at Crown Road on Sunday 7th June, 11am - 5pm!
