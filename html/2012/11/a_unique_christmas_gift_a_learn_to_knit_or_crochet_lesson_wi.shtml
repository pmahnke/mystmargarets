A unique Christmas Gift - a learn to knit or crochet lesson with Mrs Moon Yarn & Haberdashery

Give that special someone a unique Christmas gift this winter by buying them a gift voucher for a knitting or crochet lesson with Mrs Moon Yarn & Haberdashery.

Opened in 2009 by sisters Karen Miller and Susan Ritchie, Mrs Moon www.mrsmoon.co.uk is a beautiful yarn and haberdashery shop situated in St Margarets, South West London.

A stunning celebration of knitting and crochet, Mrs Moon sells all types of yarn and patterns as well as all the equipment you’ll need to make your treasured heirlooms, including books, ribbons, fastenings and beautiful ceramic buttons.

A range of classes for all levels are available in the Mrs Moon studio within the shop, where beginners can learn to knit or crochet and leave with the skills and knowledge to start their own project, or the more accomplished can brush up on their techniques to expertly finish their pieces. Mrs Moon’s teachers are very talented, down-to-earth and friendly, so if you know someone who has always wanted to learn, now is the time to treat them to a Mrs Moon Yarn & Haberdashery gift voucher. For more information and to purchase a voucher, please call the shop on 020 8744 1190.

If you are looking for an on-trend, stylish knitting or crochet project this winter, Mrs Moon have recently launched their own exclusive gorgeous knitting and crochet designs for scarves, throws and tops, perfect projects for cold winter evenings and, once completed, a unique Christmas gift idea for a loved one.

With a huge selection of beautiful yarns and accessories, including: Rowan, Debbie Bliss, Blue Sky Alpacas, Spud & Chloe, Millamia, Rooster, Fyberspates, Natural Dye Studio, Bluefaced, Austermann, House of Alistair, Knitpro and Brittany Birch, Mrs Moon’s expert staff can guide you towards your own knitting project to create a unique and personal addition to your winter wardrobe. Snoods and scarves are ever-popular for new knitters and there is a wide selection of pattern books featuring on-trend styles for larger projects. You can leave the store safe in the knowledge that customers are always welcome to pop back in for personal advice on their project if they need any extra tips or assistance. Orders can also be placed online at the Mrs Moon website www.mrsmoon.co.uk.
