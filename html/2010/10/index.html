Christmas in St Margarets 2010

The 2010 St Margarets Christmas Fair will be held on Sunday, 5th December in Crown Road.

Organised by the St Margarets Traders Association, the event starts at 11.00am and closes at 4.00pm.

Please join us for a day of family fun and entertainment…

My St Margarets Magazine - Christmas 2010 Edition

We are currently putting together the bumper Christmas edition of our local magazine!

The magazine aims to promote St Margarets and is a platform for local schools and charities to highlight their aims and achievements. If you would like to submit and article please contact Jeannine, the magazine editor on editor@mystmargarets.com
