Celebrate Football World Cup 2010 in St Margarets

Here you will find information about the local traders who are helping to celebrate the exciting Football World Cup.

Here are some World Cup Photos taken in St Margarets local pubs.

The St Margarets Tavern

The pub is already decorated with flags from all 32 countries competing in the competition in addition to lots of England bunting too!

Come and enjoy the atmosphere and good sporting spirit at your local St Margarets pub.

The pub is running a special competition with all proceeds going to Lynn’s Bowel Cancer Campaign. Simply buy your raffle tickets for Â£1.00 each for the chance to reserve your chosen table which will be available for every England match (for you and 3 of your friends). You will also get a free round of drinks for you and your guests - nip in and buy your tickets today!

In addition, the pub is aiming to provide food relating to selected countries that are playing (i.e. Mexican for when Mexico are playing and sardines for Portugal play etc).

Have you got your England match free-entry tickets yet? As a local to St Margarets you are entitled to pick-up your special local-persons entry tickets which will allow you entry to the pub for whenever England is playing a match. During England games security will be increased to ensure everyone is able to enjoy the England games in peace (there will be extra security on the doors ) so ensure your entry by picking up your “local” entry tickets from the bar today. These tickets will guarantee you to be able to enter the pub during England matches - so be sure to secure yours to avoid disappointment.

The St Margarets Pub & Tavern details

The Turks Head

Equally The Turks Head Pub will also be showing all of the matches and will have screens available in their pub garden and also in the Winchester Hall.

For weekend matches they will also be hosting a special Football World Cup BBQ.

The Turks Head Pub details

The Marble Hill

Anyone is welcome to enjoy the Football World Cup at The Marble Hill. With the continued quality of service and community atmosphere, The Marble Hill is a great place to watch football at St Margarets.

The Marble Hill has arranged a special international menu for the FIFA World Cup tournament this year. Enjoy the selection of fine foods and wine and the arrangement of special BBQ on Sundays.

There is plenty of space in the outdoor beer garden as well as indoors to enjoy all your matches with the locals.
