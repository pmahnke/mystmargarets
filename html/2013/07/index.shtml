The (not-so-secret) Secret Wardobe is Officially...OPEN

The Secret’s out! The deputy mayor, Councillor Stephen Speak, has officially opened new dress agency The Secret Wardrobe at 22c Crown Road.

Owner Susan Roster had Buck’s Fizz and homemade strawberry macaroons on hand for the small crowd who came to see his Honour and Councillor Pamela Fleming cut the ribbon and open the wardrobe!
