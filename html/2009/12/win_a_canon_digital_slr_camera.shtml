Win a Canon Digital SLR Camera! - CLOSED

PLEASE NOTE: THIS ST MARGARETS COMPETITION HAS NOW CLOSED

Thank you to everyone who entered this competition. Judging, according to the Chase Buchanan team, was exceptionally hard with so many quality entries.

Although there were many many fantastic entries for this competition, the really hard task was getting down to the top five. And, here they are, the top five:

  • 1st Place and winning entry is from Kate Poxton of St Margarets.
  • 2nd Place - “Ants Class” - Orleans Infants School, St Margarets
  • 3rd Place - Simon Baronti of Twickenham
  • 4th Place - Molly Morgan (aged 12 weeks and 3 days at time of entry submission) of St Margarets
  • 5th Place - Tom Williams of St Margarets

Well done to everyone who entered and especially, our congratulations go out to Kate.

Announced 10 Feb 2010.
