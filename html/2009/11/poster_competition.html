Instructions

This year you can download copies of the children’s poster competition templates here which means we can print less and a few trees can breathe a sigh of relief. You only need to print the first page to enter, but please take note of the terms and conditions on the second, particularly the closing date and the collection point for the finished masterpieces.

As last year there are two age categories with their own suggested poster templates - up to seven, and aged eight and above - but either version can be used by any age, all we really ask is that the finished entries are the work of children. Have fun and good luck!

  • Poster - age up to 7 years old
  • Poster - age 8 plus
